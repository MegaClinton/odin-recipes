Salad

Description

This basic chicken salad is the perfect blank canvas for any of your favorite salad ingredients.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken breast meat
  • 1 stalk celery, diced

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking pan constantly. Watch carefully, as almonds burn easily.
  3. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice and pepper in a medium bowl. Toss with chicken, almonds and celery.
  4. Serve on a bed of lettuce. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
